About Us

Bogchi Kusina first served its flavorful menu right inside the owner's home in 2023. What started as a home-based endeavor has grown into your destination for an exciting blend of classic Filipino dishes and popular Asian cuisine.

From hearty Filipino dishes to the best of Pan-Asian noodles and soups—all delivered with a commitment to the comforting, authentic flavors that began in our kitchen.
